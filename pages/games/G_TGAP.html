12 Gauges a Prayer was created as a submission for the WARLords 2023 Game Jam.

The Story

12 Gauges A prayer's original story was centered around a priest called Gage.

While attempting an exorcism, Gage is killed by the demon and sent down to hell.

This was his plan all along.

Getting older, and growing with hatred towards god's choice to allow demonic possessions to continue, Gage takes matters into hit own hands.

His target? Satan himself.
